## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Report privately so we can fix the issue before it is disclosed:

- **Email:** [YOUR_SECURITY_EMAIL]
- **Or** use GitHub's private [Security Advisories](https://docs.github.com/en/code-security/security-advisories)
  ("Report a vulnerability" in the Security tab).

Please include:

1. A description of the vulnerability and its impact
2. Steps to reproduce (proof-of-concept if possible)
3. Affected versions or components
4. Any suggested remediation

## What to Expect

| Stage | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Acknowledgement of your report | Within [X] business days |
| Initial assessment / severity triage | Within [X] business days |
| Fix or mitigation plan | Communicated after triage |
| Public disclosure | Coordinated with you after a fix ships |

We practice coordinated disclosure: we ask that you give us a reasonable window
to remediate before any public discussion, and we will credit you (with your
permission) when the fix is released.

## Scope

In scope: the application code, APIs, and infrastructure in this repository.

Out of scope (typical -- adjust for your project):

- Vulnerabilities in third-party dependencies already tracked upstream
- Social engineering, physical attacks, or denial-of-service testing
- Reports from automated scanners without a demonstrated, exploitable impact

## Recognition

We are grateful to the security researchers who help keep our users safe.
Reporters who follow this policy will be acknowledged in our release notes and
[CONTRIBUTORS.md](./CONTRIBUTORS.md) unless they request otherwise.
